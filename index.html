Evidence
Read this first. These are group-level statistical associations reported in research — averages across studied populations, not a way to diagnose or "read" any individual. Many effects are small, overlap heavily with typical gait, and depend on context, comorbidity and measurement method. This is an educational visualization, not a clinical instrument.
